Arugula Salad

Ingredients

  • arugula
  • feta
  • chia seeds
  • pesto
  • cucumbers
  • tomatoes
  • chicken (optional)

Steps

  1. Put arugula into a bowl
  2. Chop cucumbers and tomatoes into small pieces
  3. Put cucumbers, tomatoes, and all other ingredients into bowl
  4. Toss salad and enjoy!
